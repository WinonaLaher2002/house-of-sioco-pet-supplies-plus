✂️ our story

Run by people who feed, groom, and worry about their own pets too.

House of Sioco started as a small shop in Veinte Reales, Valenzuela City, and grew the same way a good pet parent's trust grows — one honest recommendation at a time. Every product on our shelves is something we'd hand to our own dogs and cats.
